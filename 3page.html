TOP 5 HANDLING
On this page we will rank the 5 cars with the best handling. Here they will be ranked by their skidpad.


5

Porsche 911 Turbo S Cabriolet

Once again we have a 911 Turbo S. But this time it's a cabrio. Although it weighs 120 kg more than the lightweight version, this machine turns better thanks to its more poised rubber. Equipped with Pirelli P Zero Tires, this car was able to achieve a skidpad of 1.11g.

Yep, you guessed it... a Porsche! The touring version of the GT3 is a beautiful car, equipped for touring. Because of this touring need - it's a perfect machine for turning.
